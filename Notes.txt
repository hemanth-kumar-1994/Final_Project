TARGET VARIABLE SELECTION
-------------------------

TITLE:
------
Predicting and Interpreting Electric Vehicle Adoption Across Countries: A Comparative Machine Learning Study

RESEARCH QUESTION:
------------------

How accurately can machine learning models predict EV adoption across countries, and which features contribute most to model predictions?

DECISION:
---------
Ev Sales share is selected as the target variable.

REASON:
-------
1. Ev sales share directly measures adoption rather than market size.
2. It allows fair comaprsion between countries of different sizes.
3. Passengr cars provide the highest coverage in the dataset:

- 55 countries
- 4,137 odservations

4.Other vehicle categories (vans, buses, trucks, 2/3 wheelers)
 have lower coverage and different adoption strategies.

 Therefore, this study focuses on :

 Parameter : EV sales share
 Mode      : cars
 Powertrain: EV





FEATURE SELECTION
-----------------
Potential explanatory variables were evaluated based on:

1. Country coverage
2. Year coverage
3. Data completeness
4. Relevance to EV adoption

SELECTED FEATURES:
------------------
1. EV Stock
   - Represents cumulative EV market size.
   - Indicates market maturity.

2. EV Stock Share
   - Represents EV penetration in the vehicle fleet.
   - More comparable across countries.

3. EV Charging Points
   - Measures charging infrastructure availability.
   - Important factor influencing EV adoption.

EXCLUDED FEATURES:
------------------
1. Battery Demand
   - Available for only 4 countries.

2. Oil Displacement
   - Available for only 4 countries.

Including these variables would significantly reduce the number of usable observations.
